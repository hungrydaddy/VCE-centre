      Joyful atmosphere

      Unlike most of the "spoon-feeding" learning atmospheres, VCE centre upholds the principle of 'happy learning', and students and teachers are more like friends, reducing the gap between students and teachers. Secondly, most of the courses support both Chinese and English teaching, thus reduces the barriers of communication between students and teachers. Finally, our community supports students even in non-class time, you can always ask questions, so that students can get an effective response in a timely manner. At the same time, the teacher is also determined to let the students fully understand rather than simple exercising. This learning model is praised by the majority of students and parents, some students even said: every day I am waiting for going to VCE centre! Tutoring is warm just like sweet-home!

      Professional experience

      VCE centre is not only famous, but tutors are armed to the teeth. James Weng - VCE specialist maths teacher, has 9 years of high mathematics experience, graduated from the Imperial College of Technology Master of Electronic Engineering brought out a number of excellent students. Steve Weng - VCE maths Methods and Physics lecturer, graduated from the University of Melbourne Biomedicine in 2010, and has a Ph.D. in philosophy. He has 10 years of Maths Methods teaching experience, and brought out many full-score students.

      Efficient learning

      Small class teaching limits the number of one single class to 4-10 people. Classes will focus on most students and some problems they encounter to give answers to corresponding issues. The advantage of one-on-one teaching is that the classroom atmosphere is more active and that students can share ideas and help them discover their own potential problems.

      Personalised model

      With One-on-one tutoring, the teacher will be based on student personal circumstances, to arrange teaching content and speed. Compared with the advantages of group lessons, teachers will be more focused on the individual students to solve the problem, given a personalised solution that will help students in a short period of time with a substantial increase in their marks.

      Flexible online tutoring

      With online tutoring, the teacher and the student connect through skype. During the online period, students can be at home or any other place, you can learn with the tutor anywhere. This, for students that are inconvenient to come to the city, is an excellent class option, and it is also available from 7 pm to 10 pm, as well as vacation time.
